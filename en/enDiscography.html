Discography

Here you will find a listing of the songs I consider to be my best work to date. A complete list of all the tracks I have created can still be found on my Youtube Channel but most of my older songs are not good enought to be a part of my portfolio, but they are still online because it shows how far I've come since I started.
They are all devided into one of three categories:
  • Solowork: Projects I have worked on completly alone.
  • Collaborations: Projects, in which I have worked together with other artists on a original song. They have either contributed melodies, whole sections of the songs or vocals.
  • Remixes: Projects, where I have taken a song from a different artist as a basis and have given it a personal note.
Feel free to browse through those to get a feeling for the kind of music I work well with and the style that I use.
